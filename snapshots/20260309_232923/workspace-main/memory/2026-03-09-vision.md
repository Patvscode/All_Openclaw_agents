## Self-Improving Research Pipeline (cascading intelligence)
- Run 1-2 research agents daily, autonomous
- Architecture (small → medium → large):
  1. **Swarm layer (0.8b x many):** Query RAG/SQL DB broadly, find candidate topics
  2. **Filter layer (2b or 4b):** Narrow to ~7 topics, provide sources/tags/summaries for each
  3. **Decision layer (122B):** Evaluate topics, choose research paths, identify what papers to find
  4. **Researcher (122B + web):** Find actual papers, conduct full research pipeline
- Self-improving: system should understand what good research looks like
- All decisions, paths, and findings feed back into the knowledge store
- Research should be informed by what we already know (RAG-first)

- Videos Pat processes are research-dense → capture everything
